Graphs

A player’s VORP and points per game show how much better they are than their designated replacement and how much (on avg) they scored a game. Most Hall of Famers have a higher VORP and PPG than the other players, but not all.

A comparison between all players’ VORP and win shares showed that most Hall of Famers have higher values than the other players.

A comparison between all players’ win shares and efficiency rating showed that the Hall of Famers have higher values than the other players.

A player’s efficiency rating and their points per game is indicative of their offensive ability. The Hall of Famers have a higher PER and PPG, with some exceptions.
